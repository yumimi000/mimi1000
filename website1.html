POSITIVE EFFECTS ___________

Economic Growth and Prosperity:

- Increased female labor force participation and equal opportunities in the workforce can boost national GDP.

- Closing gender gaps in employment, wages, and access to productive resources enhances overall economic productivity.

- Women's economic empowerment leads to higher household incomes and investments in children's health and education.

Enhanced Educational Outcomes:

- Gender parity in education, from primary to tertiary levels, improves learning outcomes for both boys and girls.

- Educated women are more likely to send their own children, especially girls, to school, creating an intergenerational cycle of empowerment.

- Greater educational attainment for women increases their earning potential and decision-making power.
